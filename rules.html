Welcome to Celestial SMP!

To ensure a fun and safe environment for everyone, please adhere to the following rules:

Rules

  1. Respect Others: Treat all players with respect. Harassment, hate speech, and bullying will not be tolerated.
  2. No Cheating: Cheating, exploiting bugs, or using hacks is strictly prohibited.
  3. No Griefing: Do not destroy or alter other players' builds without permission.
  4. Keep Chat Clean: Avoid spamming, excessive caps, and inappropriate language in chat.
  5. Follow Staff Instructions: Always listen to the staff team. They are here to help maintain a positive environment.
  6. No Advertising: Do not advertise other servers or websites without permission.
  7. Have Fun! Enjoy your time on the server and make new friends!

Note: Other rules are in Discord

Consequences

Failure to follow these rules may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on the severity of the offense.
