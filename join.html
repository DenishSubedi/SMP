Welcome to Celestial_SMP

We are excited to have you join our community! Follow the steps below to get started.

How to Join

  1. Step 1: Make sure you have Minecraft Java Edition installed.
  2. Step 2: Open Minecraft and click on "Multiplayer".
  3. Step 3: Click on "Add Server".
  4. Step 4: Enter the server address: play.celestial-smp.com
  5. Step 5: Click "Done" and select the server from your list to join!
